Our cats

Visiting Java the Cat, you are visiting a family of cats that need a house and will be happy to meet you all. We will help you through the proccess of choosing a cat, getting all the information you require, the pre-adoption procedure... We will be with you from the start till the moment your cat(s) and you are happy and together!

If you can't have a cat at home right now, we will also offer you multiple ways to spend a funny day with us and meet our family. Thanks for your visit!
